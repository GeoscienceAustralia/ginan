void Pod ( )

Use available data to estimate the precise orbit trajectories of satellites.

The Pod comprises precise models of forces and accelerations that determine the path of orbiting satellites. It can use pseudoobservations to estimate initial contitions for orbits, or propagate forward and backward from initial contidtions to produce position estimates at any point in time.
